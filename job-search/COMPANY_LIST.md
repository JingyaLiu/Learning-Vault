## Do you need hundreds of applications?

**No — not for your profile.** PhD RS is a **targeted + referral** game, not spray-and-pray.

| Source | Realistic volume | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| [PhD RS search (Lambert)](https://natolambert.com/writing/ai-phd-job-hunt) | **~30 companies** contacted → 4 offers | Heavy networking |
| [ML RS 2025 (Zharkov)](https://asmekal.github.io/blog/posts/interviews-2025-ml-research-engineer-uk) | **3–6 months**; quality > volume | Full-time search |
| [PhD conversion metrics](https://academiatoindustry.substack.com/p/the-three-numbers-that-tell-you-exactly) | **1 interview per 8–12** targeted apps | If worse → fix CV/referrals, not volume |
| General tech (AiApply 2026) | 75–150 for generic SWE | **Too high** for senior RS; use 40–80 **targeted** |

### Recommended funnel (employed, 4 months)

```
Master list:     90–100 companies  (this file)
Active pipeline: 25–40            (in process-tracker)
Applications:    30–50 total       (not 300)
Referrals:       15–25 asks        (more important than cold apps)
Parallel loops:  2–4 at peak       (Sep–Oct)
```

**Weekly pace (employed):** 3–5 **targeted** applications + 2 outreach messages — not 20 blind applies.

**When to add volume:** only if 15+ targeted apps with **zero** screens → then fix pitch/referrals first, then expand list.
