## How to pick 🏋️ practice startups / SMBs

Use **Aug** for these. Goal: calibrate interview format, not land your dream job.

| Criterion | Good practice | Bad practice |
|---|---|---|
| Size | ~30–800 people, Series A–D | 5-person seed (no process) or FAANG |
| Interview | ML coding + research chat + behavioral | Only take-home with no feedback |
| Fit | Agents, LLM product, retrieval, applied RS | Unrelated domain (e.g. pure hardware) |
| Stakes | You'd learn even if you decline offer | Your #1 choice company |
| Location | Seattle / Bellevue / remote US | Relocation required |

**Order:** practice SMB → retrieval-familiar big co → agent/FM targets → dream labs.
