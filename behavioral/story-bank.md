## Practice Protocol

1. Pick 3 questions at random
2. 2 min answer each — timer on
3. Ask: Did I actually answer the question? (Yes/No)
4. Cut 20% of words; add one number to Result

**Target:** 8 stories that cover 90% of prompts without forcing awkward fits.
